Hidden Maze Challenge

How to Play

  • Hidden maze with invisible walls
  • Move using mouse or Arrow keys (W/A/S/D)
  • Collect all keys inside the maze
  • The door unlocks only after collecting all keys
  • Hitting a hidden wall flashes red and resets you
  • Your travelled path turns dark in Levels 1–100
  • In Levels 101–150, your travelled path stays hidden
  • Round time increases with level (4–30 minutes)
